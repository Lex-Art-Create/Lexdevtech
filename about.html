MORE ABOUT OUR TEAM LEADER

I am Oma, a Pro-Wig artist. My passion for quality Wigs started when I found out that I loved making my own hair. I quit my 9-5 job to start up my own hair brand.

An open mind guide me as I explore every style-from classic to editorial. My most rewarding moments come when I have an idea in my head, then create it exactly how I imagined while sharing it with peers.

Here at my brand, our mission is to make beautiful lace pieces for women, with realistic hairlines while protecting their natural hair underneath
